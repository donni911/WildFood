Why is my pet drinking less water and having fewer bowel movements? Is this normal?

The stool of pets on a raw diet is smaller, less pungent, and occurs less frequently, indicating proper digestive tract function. In contrast, commercial pet food often contains vegetable proteins that predators cannot digest and absorb, resulting in larger, smellier, and more frequent stool.
